The arrival of AI has sharpened these questions. As the boundary between human and machine shifts, I find myself drawn not to the technology itself, but to what it reveals — about our relationship with ourselves, and with each other.

I build things to explore these questions, and to make the experience of being alive in this strange new era a little more beautiful.
